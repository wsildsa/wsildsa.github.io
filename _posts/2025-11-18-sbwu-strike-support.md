Unionized Starbucks Workers are on strike across the country and here in the Chicagoland area in Des Plaines (Lee and Oakton store) and Geneva (3rd and State), with the Aurora store (Kirk and Butterfield) joining the strike this Thursday 11/20/25.

West Suburban IL DSA is calling on community members to support Starbucks Workers United by doing the following:

1. Sign the [No Contract, No Coffee Pledge](https://starbucksworkersunited.controlshift.app/petitions/no-contract-no-coffee-pledge-to-act-in-solidarity-with-starbucks-workers-united)
2. Show up at the picket line to support the baristas (pick dates below on form!)
3. Donate to the [Regional Strike Fund](https://www.gofundme.com/f/illinois-sbwu-regional-strike-fund) to support SBWU workers directly while they are out on strike
4. Post about the strike on social media
5. Collect and distribute non-perishable foods and home essentials for striking workers
6. Prepare breakfast or lunch and bring it to the picket line

Fill out the survey below and let us know how you can help support!
